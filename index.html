Welcome to my Data Analyst Portfolio

This site will showcase a number of data related projects I have completed while studying a Bachelor of Business Analytics at Macquarie University in Sydney Australia.
The projects cover topics relating to business forcasting and modeling, statistical analysis and hypothesis testing, relational database design, data cleaning and wrangling, data visualisation and machine learning models.
I use a variety of tools including Excel, Python, R, SQL, PowerBI and Tableau.
